01 — About

Who
I Am.

I'm a Class 12 CBSE graduate from Delhi with a knack for building things — whether that's a Python health tracker, an electromagnetic induction model, or shooting with a full-frame camera.

My interests live at the crossroads of hardware and software. I've done Arduino projects, studied ECE-level circuits, and built software from the ground up. I think in systems — how things connect, why they work, and how to make them better.

Outside of tech, I'm into cars, PC hardware, photography, and gaming. The same obsessive attention to detail applies to all of it.
